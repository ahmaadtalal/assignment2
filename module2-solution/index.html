Our Menu

Chicken

Our chicken dishes are crafted with the finest ingredients to ensure a delicious and satisfying meal. From classic grilled chicken to spicy buffalo wings, our menu offers a variety of options to suit every taste. Try our signature chicken parmesan, featuring tender breaded chicken breast topped with marinara sauce and melted mozzarella cheese.

Beef

Indulge in our savory beef selections, prepared to perfection. Our beef menu includes everything from juicy steaks to hearty beef stews. Don't miss our famous beef bourguignon, a traditional French dish made with succulent pieces of beef, slow-cooked in red wine with mushrooms, onions, and bacon.

Sushi

Experience the freshness and artistry of our sushi offerings. We use only the highest quality seafood and ingredients to create an array of sushi rolls, nigiri, and sashimi. Our chef's special roll, the Dragon Roll, features crispy tempura shrimp, avocado, and cucumber, topped with eel and a sweet eel sauce.
